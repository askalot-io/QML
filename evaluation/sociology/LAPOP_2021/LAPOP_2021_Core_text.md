NOTES:
1. Questions highlighted in light blue were not included in all countries. Check country-specific questtionaires.
2. Experimental modules (highlighted in gray) are considered supplemental to the AmericasBarometer, and not released for a minimum of two years, after that period interested parties can contact LAPOP with questions about how to access these data.
3. The questionnaire was a split-sample design: approximately half the respondents were randomly assigned to "Core A" (questions marked in this questionnaire with CA) and about half randomly assigned to "Core B" (questions marked in this questionnaire with CB). Questions marked with a "T" were included in both questionnaires (Core A and Core B). Each dataset contains a variable called "core_a_core_b", it distinguishes the questionnaire that was applied to each respondent.
